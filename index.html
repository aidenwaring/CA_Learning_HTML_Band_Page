Tycho

Scott Hansen, known professionally as Tycho, is an American musician, composer, songwriter and producer based in San Francisco. He is also known as ISO50 for his photographic and design work. His music blends multiple stylistic components, including downtempo guitar, analogue synthesis, and ambient elements such as breathing and found sounds of weather broadcasts and dialogue.
